What We Do

At Go Green Auto Recycling we recover all of the hazardous materials within your car or truck and separately harvest all the various components for recycling. Our goal is to drastically reduce the amount of contaminants and waste that affect our environment, by recycling cars correctly.

What normally happens to vehicles when they reach their end-of-life is they are taken to salvage yards where they sit rusting away for many years. These cars or vehicles can no longer be used because they are unable to be repaired, or they are no longer of use due to being severely damaged in an accident, a fire, etc.

How we recycle your vehicle

  • All hazardous liquids are drained and contained. This includes fuel, engine oil, transmission fluid, engine coolant. Refrigerant is recovered if applicable.
  • Wheels and tires are separated and can be fully re-purposed or recycled.
  • Wiring and electrical parts are all removed and separated for recycling.
  • Batteries & catalytic converters are sent to recovery and recycling.
  • Non Ferris metal components like those made of aluminum are removed.
  • Remaining Ferris metals are sent to a steel re-smelting facility.
